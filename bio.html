Biography

Xiaoqi Yu is a PhD Candidate in Finance at the University of Glasgow, Adam Smith Business School. He will be on the academic job market in 2026. His research focuses on Asset Pricing, Capital Market Efficiency, and Sustainable Finance. He holds the CFA and FRM designations.

Prior to his doctoral studies, he completed an MSc in Quantitative Finance (with Distinction) at the University of Glasgow and a BEc in Finance at Shanghai Maritime University. He also has professional experience in investment research and equity research in China's financial industry.
